Breakfast Recipes

Start your morning with energy and flavor.

Fluffy Pancakes

Soft, airy, and golden pancakes for a perfect breakfast.

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 2 tablespoons sugar
  3. 1 tablespoon baking powder
  4. 1/2 teaspoon salt


Blueberry Muffins

Moist and fluffy muffins filled with juicy blueberries.

  1. 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 3/4 cup sugar
  3. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. 2 teaspoons baking powder


Avocado Toast

Healthy and delicious toast with fresh avocado.

  1. 2 slices whole-grain bread
  2. 1 ripe avocado
  3. Salt and pepper to taste
  4. 1 teaspoon lemon juice


Scrambled Eggs

Creamy scrambled eggs cooked to perfection with butter and herbs.

  1. 4 large eggs
  2. 2 tablespoons milk
  3. Salt and pepper to taste
  4. 1 tablespoon butter


Fruit Parfait

Layers of yogurt, granola, and fresh fruits for a colorful and healthy start.

  1. 1 cup Greek yogurt
  2. 1/2 cup granola
  3. 1/2 cup mixed fresh fruits (berries, banana, mango)
  4. 1 tablespoon honey


Oatmeal Bowl

Warm oats topped with honey, banana, and cinnamon.

  1. 1 cup rolled oats
  2. 2 cups milk or water
  3. 1 banana, sliced
  4. 1 tablespoon honey


Banana Pancakes

Soft banana pancakes drizzled with honey and topped with whipped cream.

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 1 ripe banana, mashed
  3. 1 tablespoon sugar
  4. 1 teaspoon baking powder


Yogurt Parfait

Smooth yogurt mixed with fruits and topped with crunchy granola.

  1. 1 cup plain yogurt
  2. 1/2 cup mixed fresh fruits (berries, kiwi, pineapple)
  3. 1/4 cup granola
  4. 1 tablespoon honey


Breakfast Burrito

Warm tortilla filled with eggs, cheese, and vegetables for a hearty breakfast.

  1. 1 large flour tortilla
  2. 2 large eggs, scrambled
  3. 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  4. 1/4 cup diced vegetables (bell peppers, onions, tomatoes)
