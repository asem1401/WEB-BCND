Dinner Recipes

Perfect dishes to end your day with joy.

Grilled Steak

Perfectly seasoned and grilled to perfection.

  1. Season steak with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Preheat grill to high heat.
  3. Grill steak for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.


Fettuccine Alfredo

Creamy pasta in a rich Parmesan and butter sauce.

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter and add heavy cream.
  3. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted.
  4. Toss pasta in the sauce and serve hot.


Beshbarmak

Traditional Kazakh dish with tender meat, noodles and broth.

  1. Boil meat (lamb or beef) until tender, reserving the broth.
  2. Prepare dough and roll into thin sheets, then cut into noodles.
  3. Cook noodles in the meat broth until tender.
  4. Serve meat over noodles, garnished with onions and herbs.


Herb-Crusted Lamb Chops

Tender lamb chops coated with herbs and served with roasted garlic

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix breadcrumbs, parsley, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush lamb chops with mustard and coat with herb mixture.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes for medium-rare. Let rest before serving.


Seafood Paella

Spanish rice dish with shrimp, mussels, saffron, and lemon zest.

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and bell peppers in olive oil.
  2. Add rice and toast for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in saffron-infused broth and cook until rice is almost done.
  4. Add seafood and cook until just done. Garnish with lemon wedges.


Moroccan Tagine

Slow-cooked chicken with apricots, almonds, and warm spices.

  1. Brown chicken pieces in a tagine or heavy pot.
  2. Add onions, garlic, ginger, cinnamon, cumin, and turmeric.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and add apricots and almonds.
  4. Cover and simmer for 1.5-2 hours until chicken is tender.


Honey-Glazed Salmon

Baked salmon brushed with honey soy glaze and sesame seeds.

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix honey, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger for the glaze.
  3. Brush salmon fillets with glaze and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes until salmon is cooked through.


Risotto with Asparagus and Lemon

Creamy Italian risotto with fresh asparagus and lemon zest.

  1. Sauté onions in olive oil until translucent.
  2. Add Arborio rice and toast for 2 minutes.
  3. Gradually add warm broth, stirring constantly until absorbed.
  4. Stir in blanched asparagus, lemon zest, and Parmesan cheese.


Black Garlic Pasta

Homemade pasta tossed with black garlic, olive oil, and parmesan.

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté minced black garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  3. Toss cooked pasta in the garlic oil and mix in grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh herbs before serving.
