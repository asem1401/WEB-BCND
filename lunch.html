Lunch Recipes

Tasty meals to keep you going through the day.

Caesar Salad

Classic salad with crispy croutons and parmesan.

  1. 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  2. 1/2 cup croutons
  3. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  4. 1/3 cup Caesar dressing


Caprese Sandwich

Fresh mozzarella, tomato, and basil sandwich.

  1. 2 slices ciabatta bread
  2. 4 slices fresh mozzarella
  3. 2 slices tomato
  4. Fresh basil leaves


Chicken Noodle Soup

Comforting soup with tender chicken and vegetables.

  1. 1 lb chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  2. 6 cups chicken broth
  3. 2 cups egg noodles
  4. 1 cup chopped carrots and celery


Beef Stir-Fry

Tender beef sautéed with colorful bell peppers and soy sauce.

  1. 1/2 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  2. 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  3. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  4. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil


Shrimp Tacos

Soft tortillas filled with spicy shrimp, avocado, and fresh salsa.

  1. 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  2. 4 small tortillas
  3. 1 avocado, sliced
  4. 1/2 cup fresh salsa


Ramen Bowl

Japanese noodle soup with soft-boiled egg, tender pork, and miso broth.

  1. 2 packs ramen noodles
  2. 4 cups miso broth
  3. 1 soft-boiled egg, halved
  4. 1/2 cup sliced pork belly


Shakshuka with Pita

Eggs poached in a rich tomato sauce with spices, served with pita bread.

  1. 4 large eggs
  2. 2 cups tomato sauce
  3. 1 teaspoon cumin
  4. 4 pita breads


Mango Chicken Curry

A sweet and spicy curry with mango, coconut milk, and tender chicken.

  1. 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  2. 1 cup mango chunks
  3. 1 can coconut milk
  4. 2 tablespoons curry powder


Truffle Mushroom Risotto

Creamy Italian rice infused with truffle oil and wild mushrooms.

  1. 1 cup Arborio rice
  2. 4 cups vegetable broth
  3. 1 cup mixed wild mushrooms
  4. 1 tablespoon truffle oil
